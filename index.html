About Us

  • Mar 15

    Resource Finder Launched

    Our platform went live, connecting those in need with essential resources in the community.

    First Restaurant Partnership

    Local diner "Hearty Meals" joined our network, offering free meals during off-peak hours.

    Shelter Network Expansion

    "Safe Haven Shelter" became our first partnered shelter, providing emergency housing options.
